Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of flat, wide pasta alternated with rich meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese, and often mozzarella or Parmesan. The sauce typically includes ground meat, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs like basil and oregano. Each layer is assembled in a baking dish and cooked until bubbling and golden on top, resulting in a hearty, flavorful casserole that showcases a blend of textures and savory tastes.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato products
  • Onion and garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Sugar
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
